I am an applied economist working in Economic History, Development and Political Economy. I am currently a research fellow at IAST - Toulouse School of Economics and will join King's College London as an Assistant Professor (Department of Political Economy) in 2025.

My current projects explore the lasting consequences of violence during colonial slavery in the Carribean, and gender-based violence in contemporary settings. I use state-of-the-art AI methods for document processing and understanding.
